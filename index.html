About

I’m a third-year Biomedical Engineering undergraduate at the University of Moratuwa, focused on biosignal processing, medical instrumentation, and machine learning for healthcare.

I build end-to-end biomedical systems — from sensor integration and signal conditioning to data analysis and intelligent models. My goal is to translate physiological data into meaningful, accessible healthcare solutions.

I value clarity, structured problem-solving, and collaborative work. My interest in reading and writing strengthens how I communicate technical ideas.

Core Strengths

  • Biosignals: ECG/PPG analysis and signal workflows
  • Medical Devices: Sensor interfacing and embedded prototyping
  • Machine Learning: Classification and healthcare ML integration
  • Systems Thinking: Bridging hardware, software, and users
